Duke Develence is a nobleman and member of Elturel's ruling Tribunal following the city's return from the Hells.

Unlike several of his fellow Tribunal members, Develence appeared relatively sympathetic toward Orpheus Tchazzum despite his distrust of Apollo and the Tchazzum family.

During the party's appearance before the Tribunal, Develence played a quieter role than some of the harsher officials present, though his influence within Elturel's political structure remains substantial.

He continues serving among the leadership attempting to stabilize the fractured city after the Descent.
