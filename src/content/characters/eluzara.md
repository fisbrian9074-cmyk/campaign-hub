Eluzara traveled with the party during the period immediately following the events of Stormwreck Isle.

Little is currently known about her origins, though she possessed notable magical talent and was present during the group's journey through Greenest.

During a sudden and violent magical storm outside the city, Eluzara vanished alongside the kobold Myla without warning. The event left behind magical devastation and severely injured the wizard Timmn.

Neither Eluzara nor Myla have been seen since the storm.
