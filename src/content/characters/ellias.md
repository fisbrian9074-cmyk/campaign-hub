A former legal assistant employed by the powerful Tchazzum family of Elturel, Elias Viremont became entangled in a sprawling infernal conspiracy after uncovering suspicious financial records tied to Apollo Tchazzum and his associates. After confronting Apollo and attempting to expose evidence of corruption and cult activity, Elias was dismissed from the estate and forced to survive in the slums surrounding the recovering city.

Grounded, analytical, and morally driven, Elias often serves as the party's voice of reason in an increasingly chaotic world shaped by divine politics, infernal influence, and magical instability. Though not a traditional warrior, his knowledge of legal systems, finance, and political maneuvering has repeatedly revealed hidden truths behind the growing conspiracy surrounding the Broken Weave.

Elias is haunted by the loss of his wife, Corrinth, who disappeared during the Descent of Elturel. Unknown to most, Corrinth survived — transformed into the terrifying Black Iron Acolyte after her damaged body was reconstructed using infernal iron by unknown forces. Elias continues searching desperately for a way to save her while carrying several dangerous artifacts and pieces of evidence tied to the Tchazzum conspiracy.

Among the items in his possession are demonic correspondence between Apollo Tchazzum and Dak, fragments of infernal iron that react violently to nearby magic, and a mysterious latent magic stone discovered after surviving Corrinth's massacre in Triel.
