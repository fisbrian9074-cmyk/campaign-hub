Trolfast is a high-ranking paladin serving the Triad leadership of Elturel.

Distinguished by braided hair and a stern military bearing, Trolfast represents the god Tyr within the Tribunal and serves as one of the primary defenders of Elturel's post-Descent order.

Like many within the city's new leadership, Trolfast believes maintaining stability through strength and decisive action is necessary to prevent another catastrophe.

Though loyal to the Triad, his exact stance regarding the Tchazzum family and the growing conspiracies beneath Elturel remains uncertain.
