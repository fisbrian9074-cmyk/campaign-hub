Apollo Tchazzum is the patriarch of the wealthy Tchazzum family and one of the central figures tied to the growing conspiracy surrounding Elturel, Triel, and the Broken Weave.

Originally based in Waterdeep, Apollo relocated his family and fortune to Elturel shortly after the city's return from the Hells. There, he rapidly established a lavish estate within one of the city's recovering districts despite evidence suggesting his finances had once been on the verge of collapse.

Financial records uncovered by Elias Viremont and Pixeltor revealed a sudden influx of wealth roughly five years earlier, followed by increasingly elaborate spending patterns and suspicious financial manipulation through dummy accounts and hidden investments across multiple cities.

Apollo has been linked through hidden correspondence to Dak, infernal operations involving Assets L and H, and the mysterious ritual known as the Tempering. However, recent discoveries suggest Apollo himself may be suffering from altered or removed memories following a ritual of purification conducted by Father Henry after the events surrounding Eurydice and his former wife.

Though manipulative and politically dangerous, Apollo has recently shown signs of genuine regret regarding his treatment of Orpheus and the choices that led to his family's downfall.
