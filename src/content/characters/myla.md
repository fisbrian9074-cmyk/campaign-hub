Myla is a small kobold spellcaster associated with Dragon's Rest upon Stormwreck Isle. Standing roughly three feet tall, she carries numerous magical ingredients and alchemical vials along her belt while wielding a staff adorned with a living scorpion that she regularly feeds.

Gifted — or cursed — with unpredictable Wild Magic, Myla often displayed chaotic magical surges capable of producing dangerous and unexpected effects.

Following the defeat of Sparkrender and the party's departure from Stormwreck Isle, Myla disappeared during a violent magical storm near Greenest alongside the mage Eluzara. She may have briefly appeared in the Berdusk prison as Gorruk made his famous escape, but she was in a jail cell adjacent to him and he could not confirm her I.D. 100%

Her current whereabouts remain unknown.

She was a fan favorite
