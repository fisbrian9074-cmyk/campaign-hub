Leto is the teenage son of Lady Tabitha and stepson of Apollo Tchazzum.

Though not related to Apollo by blood, Leto bears a striking resemblance to the Tchazzum patriarch, a similarity that several members of the party found deeply unsettling.

Approaching adulthood and eager to prove himself, Leto trains daily with the estate's guards and aspires to become a skilled swordsman. He greatly admires Orpheus and views him as both an older brother and a role model.

Despite his youth, Leto appears surprisingly intelligent and observant regarding the political tensions surrounding the Tchazzum estate.

His future role within the unfolding conspiracy remains unclear.
