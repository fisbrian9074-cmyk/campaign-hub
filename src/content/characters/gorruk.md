Gorruk "Dragonbane" Thorne was a towering half-orc mercenary and former member of the Bloodaxe Company known for his immense physical strength, fearlessness in battle, and surprisingly loyal heart.

Scarred from years of warfare and driven by a complicated past, Gorruk traveled alongside the party from the earliest days of Stormwreck Isle through the liberation of Berdusk and the growing unrest surrounding Elturel.

Despite his intimidating appearance and violent tendencies, Gorruk formed strong bonds with his companions and often acted as the group's frontline protector. He possessed extensive knowledge of mercenary culture, battlefield tactics, and monstrous creatures gathered during years of campaigning across the region.

During the party's journey toward Elturel, Gorruk was killed in battle against a massive roc that attacked the group near Fort Morninglord. Even after being carried into the sky by the creature, Gorruk managed to mortally wound it before both warrior and beast plummeted violently back to the earth.

The impact shattered Gorruk's body, destroyed his beloved greataxe, and left the surviving members of the party devastated. His death became one of the defining emotional moments of the campaign.

Though remembered for his brutality in combat, Gorruk is equally remembered for his loyalty to his friends, his crude humor, and his refusal to back down from impossible odds. He also may have been a slight alcoholic.
