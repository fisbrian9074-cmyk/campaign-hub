Pixeltor is an elderly spellcaster with a booming beard streaked with brown and grey hair. Heavy set and often socially awkward, he spent most of his life isolated within the Frostfang Forest, dedicating himself to woodworking, magical study, and protecting the wilderness from outside threats.

Though often eccentric and unconventional, Pixeltor has repeatedly proven himself to be one of the party's most reliable magical assets. His spells and inventions have saved the group countless times, including during the assassination of General Strongsword in Triel.

Pixeltor struggles with societal norms and interpersonal communication, often relying on humor and odd behavior to navigate tense situations. Despite this, his loyalty to his companions is unquestionable.

Following the liberation of Berdusk and later events surrounding Triel and Elturel, Pixeltor became increasingly tied to the growing magical instability known as the Broken Weave. He currently possesses several dwarf-crafted magical artifacts, including a burning flower amulet which reacts violently to infernal iron and demonic influence.

Pixeltor's favorite color is the orange of autumn leaves, a reflection of his deep connection to the forests and wildlands he calls home.
