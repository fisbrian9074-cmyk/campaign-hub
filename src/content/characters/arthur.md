Arthur was once believed to be nothing more than the village drunk and local madman haunting the settlement of Hornhollow.

Wrapped in ragged black clothing and constantly muttering to himself while sketching spiraled vegetables into a worn notebook, Arthur was largely ignored by the townsfolk.

After the party cleared the Haunted Ruins and broke the curse afflicting the region, Arthur's true identity was revealed. Decades earlier, he had been a Purple Dragon Knight from the eastern kingdom of Cormyr, sent alongside a company of soldiers to purge the ruins. Arthur was the sole survivor.

The horrors he experienced left his mind shattered for nearly sixty years.

Freed from the curse at last, Arthur regained his senses and swore loyalty to the party, promising to answer their call for aid should they ever require assistance before returning to his homeland.

Another fan favorite, Arthur was well recieved by the boys
