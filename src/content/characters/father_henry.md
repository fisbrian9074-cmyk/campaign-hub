Father Henry is a reverend devoted to Ilmater, the god associated with suffering, endurance, and mercy. Though outwardly warm and approachable — often laughing at Pixeltor's jokes during Tribunal proceedings — Henry possesses connections to several deeply suspicious events tied to the Tchazzum family.

According to Apollo Tchazzum's personal journal, Henry once conducted a ritual of purification intended to help Apollo cope with the guilt surrounding Eurydice, Orpheus, and Apollo's former wife. Following the ritual, Apollo described an unnatural sense of relief and emotional detachment, suggesting that memories or emotions may have been magically altered or removed.

Father Henry remains an influential member of the Triad leadership within Elturel and may possess knowledge regarding the Tempering, infernal corruption, and the deeper consequences of the Descent.
