Vheamena is a devil originating from the infernal plane of Avernus. She possesses fiery red skin, golden eyes, long black hair, and massive bat-like wings extending from her shoulders.

She served as the infernal patron of Kaithus following the death of his parents during his childhood, maintaining influence over his life and decisions for years.

After the fall of Sparkrender on Stormwreck Isle, Vheamena appeared before the party unexpectedly. Though disappointed Kaithus had failed to reach his intended destination, she expressed admiration for the group's ability to slay a dragon.

Vheamena subsequently teleported the party far from the island and instructed them to travel north and meet a contact known as Dak, unknowingly placing the adventurers onto the path that would eventually lead toward the Broken Weave conspiracy.
