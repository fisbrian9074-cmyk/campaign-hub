Dak is a mysterious assassin and operative tied to multiple conspiracies spanning Stormwreck Isle, Berdusk, Elturel, and Triel.

First encountered among the soldiers of Tyrin Dysir near Greenest, Dak demonstrated the ability to communicate telepathically and possessed prior knowledge regarding Kaithus and the party. He repeatedly appeared behind major incidents throughout the campaign before disappearing again without explanation.

Evidence recovered by Elias Viremont later revealed correspondence between Dak and Apollo Tchazzum discussing the deployment of infernal Assets and preparations for the ritual known as the Tempering.

Dak has impersonated civilians, manipulated local leadership, committed assassinations, and coordinated hidden operations tied to Zarielite activity throughout the region. Despite repeated encounters, very little is known about his true motives, allegiances, or origins.
