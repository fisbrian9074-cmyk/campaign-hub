Varnoth is a battle-scarred orc woman residing at Dragon's Rest following a long life spent fighting as a mercenary commander in northern lands.

She possesses dark green skin, short black hair cropped close to her head, and an elegant prosthetic replacing one of her legs. Countless faded scars cover her body, evidence of decades spent surviving brutal warfare.

Seeking peace after years of violence, Varnoth retired to Stormwreck Isle where she assisted Runara and the cloister's inhabitants. Despite her retirement, she retained a sharp tactical mind and frequently offered practical guidance to the party.

Varnoth was among the first people to witness the adventurers grow from inexperienced wanderers into genuine heroes.
