Ddiphis Acoria is a dragonborn warrior who became a recurring ally of the party following the liberation of Berdusk.

Recognizable by the strange sigil she bears — a flaming winged mistress — Ddiphis operates largely alone after separating from her former associates. She first approached the party after detecting strange lights and suspicious activity within the swamps surrounding Berdusk.

Practical, disciplined, and unusually comfortable eating raw eggs for breakfast, Ddiphis quickly proved herself a capable fighter and investigator. She maintained connections to Lionel Methermash and possessed knowledge regarding cult movements and hidden operations spreading through Elturgard following the return of Elturel.

Ddiphis later traveled alongside Varrik, David, and Therus during the Triel crisis before separating from the group after the assassination of General Strongsword.
