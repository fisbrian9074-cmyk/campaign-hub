Justiar Echthelion is a severe and uncompromising official serving the Tribunal of Elturel.

During the party's appearance before the Tribunal following the liberation of Berdusk, Echthelion aggressively questioned the group regarding their actions, particularly the killing of Cierra Riv and the destruction left in their wake.

Firmly devoted to order, justice, and preserving Elturel from further disaster, Echthelion views uncontrolled adventurers and political instability as direct threats to the fragile peace established after the Descent.

His distrust of the party was evident from their very first meeting.
