Commander Phaltarrion serves as one of Elturel's chief intelligence officers within the Triad's growing political structure.

Cold, observant, and deeply involved in the city's covert operations, Phaltarrion played a major role in assigning the party to investigate Triel during the early stages of the crisis.

Though officially acting in the interests of peacekeeping and restoring order, Phaltarrion's methods and secretive nature have caused several members of the party to distrust him.

At one point, the party stole confidential notes connected to his investigations, potentially exposing hidden information regarding cult infiltration, military deployments, and the expanding conspiracy surrounding the Broken Weave.
