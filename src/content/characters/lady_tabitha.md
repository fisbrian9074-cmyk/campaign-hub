Lady Tabitha is Apollo Tchazzum's second wife, entering the family shortly after Apollo's divorce from his first wife.

Born into a once-prominent noble bloodline ruined by gambling debt and political disgrace, Tabitha married Apollo during the years preceding the family's relocation to Elturel.

Though outwardly composed and aristocratic, little is currently known about her true motivations or involvement in the darker activities surrounding the Tchazzum estate.

She is the mother of Leto and maintains a carefully controlled public image within Elturel's recovering noble circles.
