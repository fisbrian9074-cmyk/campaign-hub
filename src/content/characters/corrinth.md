Corrinth is the missing wife of Elias Viremont, believed lost during the Descent of Elturel when the city was dragged into the Hells. Unknown to Elias for much of the campaign, Corrinth survived the catastrophe through horrifying means.

During the party's assault on Governoress Priseris's Manor in Triel, the mysterious Black Iron Acolyte revealed herself to be Corrinth beneath her helm. Her body had been partially reconstructed using infernal iron, granting her terrifying strength and resilience while seemingly binding her to infernal forces connected to the Tempering.

Corrinth single-handedly defeated the party during the battle in the Manor and executed the Governoress, an act which unknowingly helped pave the way for Tchazzum influence to expand within the region. Despite overwhelming the party, Corrinth spared their lives and abandoned them in the city square under mysterious circumstances.

Fragments of Corrinth's humanity may still remain beneath the infernal corruption, a fact Elias desperately clings to as he searches for a way to save her.
