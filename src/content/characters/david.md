David was a towering goliath warrior known for his stone-grey skin, tribal tattoos, and near-constant access to alcohol.

Despite his drunken demeanor and laid-back personality, David repeatedly fought alongside Varrik and the party against cult forces, military occupation, and infernal threats spreading throughout the region.

David participated in the operations surrounding Triel and the assassination of General Strongsword. During the confrontation following Strongsword's death, David was killed in action.

His death marked one of the first major losses suffered by the group during the escalating conflict surrounding the Broken Weave.
