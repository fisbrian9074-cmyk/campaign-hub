Tarak is a middle-aged human trader who operates the trading post at Dragon's Rest upon Stormwreck Isle.

Recognizable by his tan skin, auburn hair, greying beard, and faded tattoos, Tarak initially appeared to be nothing more than a rugged merchant supplying travelers and visitors to the cloister. Closer inspection, however, reveals markings identifying him as a former member of the Gilded Gallows — a notorious thieves' guild operating within Elturgard.

Despite his criminal past, Tarak proved dependable and resourceful during the party's time on Stormwreck Isle. He later assisted the group by crafting restorative items using rare materials recovered from the island's caverns.

Though generally relaxed and practical, Tarak possesses extensive knowledge of smuggling routes, criminal networks, and regional rumors.
