Tyrin Dysir is a scarred human mercenary commander with blonde hair, grey eyes, and a hardened military demeanor forged through years of warfare.

Formerly Gorruk's commanding officer within the Bloodaxe Company, Tyrin later appeared near Greenest operating alongside forces loyal to General Ilvessar Ältariel.

Though stern and practical, Tyrin earned respect from those who served under him through battlefield competence and disciplined leadership. His growing association with military powers connected to Elturel and Ilvessar has raised concerns among those wary of the increasing authoritarian influence spreading throughout the region after the Descent.

Tyrin unknowingly housed Dak among his soldiers during one of the party's earliest encounters with the mysterious operative.
