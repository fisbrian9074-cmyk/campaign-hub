Runara is an ancient bronze dragon who resides upon Stormwreck Isle disguised as an elderly human woman with tanned skin, white hair, and hazel eyes.

As caretaker of the cloister known as Dragon's Rest, Runara guided travelers, sheltered survivors, and oversaw the temple dedicated to Bahamut located upon the island. Wise and patient, she played an important role in helping the party during the earliest days of their adventures.

Runara understood the dangers surrounding Stormwreck Isle long before the party arrived and quietly monitored the growing draconic instability caused by Sparkrender and other forces connected to the island.

Though gentle in demeanor, Runara possesses the immense wisdom and power expected of an ancient dragon devoted to preserving balance and protecting mortal lives.
