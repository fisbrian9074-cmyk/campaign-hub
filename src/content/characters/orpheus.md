The son of Apollo Tchazzum and heir to one of the most influential noble families tied to post-Descent Elturel, Orpheus abandoned his family's wealth and influence years ago under mysterious circumstances involving his wife, Eurydice. During his travels, he became a capable adventurer and eventually joined the Heroes of Berdusk.

Orpheus possesses a strained and deeply complicated relationship with his father. Though he long believed Apollo to be cruel and manipulative, recent discoveries suggest Apollo himself may have undergone supernatural memory alteration tied to rituals performed by Father Henry and the Triad.

During the chaos surrounding Triel and the growing influence of Shar, Orpheus consumed a mysterious potion intended to aid his wife. Instead, the potion violently erased his memories, leaving him vulnerable to manipulation and fragmented recollections of his past. Since then, Orpheus has slowly regained pieces of his identity while struggling with visions, divine influence, and growing suspicions surrounding his family.

Despite his amnesia, Orpheus remains one of the group's most capable combatants and a central figure in the unfolding conspiracy surrounding the Tempering, the return of Elturel, and the hidden plans of the Tchazzum family.
