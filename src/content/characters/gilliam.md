Gilliam is the elderly butler serving the Tchazzum family estate in Elturel.

Short in stature and recognizable by his mustache and formal demeanor, Gilliam initially appeared to be a loyal servant dedicated entirely to maintaining the estate and caring for its residents.

However, following the party's return from Triel, several strange inconsistencies emerged in Gilliam's behavior. He appeared unable to remember major confrontations that had previously taken place within the estate, including violent disputes involving Orpheus and Leto.

Though he physically appeared unchanged, Elias and the others noted that something about Gilliam felt subtly wrong, as though parts of his memory or personality had been altered.

Whether this condition is connected to Father Henry's rituals, Shar's influence, or the broader magical instability surrounding the Broken Weave remains unknown.

He seems to possess the power of teleporting, or duplicating himself, or something similar based on recent events.
