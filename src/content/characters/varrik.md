Varrik is a black dragonborn adventurer and former operative of the Lord's Alliance who became one of the party's earliest and most reliable allies.

His black scales possess an unusual wet sheen, while pale white patches across his face create the appearance of exposed bone beneath the skin. Intelligent, resourceful, and deeply suspicious of growing cult influence throughout the region, Varrik spent much of the campaign investigating the activities of the Order of the Lady of Iron, Zarielite infiltrators, and political corruption tied to Elturel.

Varrik first directed the party toward Berdusk where they uncovered and defeated Cierra Riv. Later, during the growing crisis in Triel, he worked alongside David and Therus attempting to expose Zariel cult activity without fully destroying the city.

Following the assault on Governoress Priseris's Manor, Varrik disappeared. He was subsequently blamed for the attack and forced into hiding while many factions sought him for questioning or execution.
