---
title: The Death of Strongsword
session_number: 18
date: 2026-04-23T19:00:00.000-04:00
summary: The party assassinates General Strongsword, triggering devastating consequences.
---

The party confronted General Strongsword during the height of the conflict surrounding Triel.

After a brutal battle, Pixeltor delivered the killing blow against the general.

In the aftermath, Ilvessar mourned Strongsword openly and condemned the party's actions, describing the countless lives the general had once saved.

The confrontation escalated into violence when Ilvessar attacked Orpheus while invoking memories of Apollo's abuse and manipulation.

Orpheus ultimately killed Ilvessar.

During the chaos, David was also killed in action.

Following the battle, Ddiphis and Therus separated from the group while the surviving party members collected payment for the assassinations before leaving Triel behind.
