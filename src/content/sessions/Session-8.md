The party was approached by Ddiphis Acoria, a dragonborn warrior concerned about mysterious lights and possible infiltration near Berdusk.

Traveling into the swampy wilderness outside the city, the group encountered strange teleporting creatures marked with glowing blue symbols.

The expedition led the adventurers into dangerous territory occupied by kobolds and deceptive panther-like beasts capable of teleportation.

The session ended with the party hearing another creature rapidly approaching through the darkness.
