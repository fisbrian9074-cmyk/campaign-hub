---
title: Orders for Triel
session_number: 15
date: 2025-09-26T19:00:00.000-04:00
summary: Hidden documents reveal the true scale of Elturel's military deployment toward Triel.
---

While preparing for departure toward Triel, the party uncovered confidential military information connected to Commander Phaltarrion and the Tribunal.

The documents revealed that Elturel had massively understated the number of troops being sent to the city.

Rather than the five hundred soldiers originally claimed, nearly three thousand troops had been mobilized under General Strongsword's command.

Additional notes referenced mysterious "Assets" operating within cities throughout the region and specifically mentioned Varrik Battleborne as an obstacle requiring attention.

Realizing the political situation was far worse than expected, the party departed Elturel and began their journey toward Triel.
