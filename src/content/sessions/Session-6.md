Under instructions connected to Varrik and the Lord's Alliance, the party traveled to Berdusk to investigate growing instability surrounding the Order of the Lady of Iron.

The city was already on the edge of collapse when the adventurers arrived.

During their investigation, the party confronted General Cierra Riv, an elven leader attempting to bind the Material Plane together with the Feywild.

After defeating Riv, the group became celebrated as the Heroes of Berdusk. They were awarded land, property, and noble recognition outside the city walls.

Though victorious, rumors across Elturgard suggested larger threats were still growing beyond Berdusk's borders.
