This session marked the introduction of Elias Viremont, former legal assistant to the Tchazzum family.

The party appeared before the Tribunal of Elturel, where officials questioned them aggressively regarding the destruction surrounding Berdusk and the death of Cierra Riv.

Several powerful political figures were present during the proceedings, including Duke Develence, Trolfast, Justiar Echthelion, Father Henry, and Commander Phaltarrion.

Though suspicious of the party, the Tribunal ultimately assigned them a new mission: travel to the unstable city of Triel and assist General Strongsword in restoring order amid rumors of rebellion and cult infiltration.

Following the hearing, Orpheus reunited with his father, Apollo Tchazzum, for the first time in years.
