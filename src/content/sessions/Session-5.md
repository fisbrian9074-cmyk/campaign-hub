---

While investigating ancient ruins connected to a forgotten deity, the party encountered deadly traps, hostile creatures, and supernatural forces.

During the expedition, Kaithus was killed.

His death marked the first major tragedy suffered by the group and deeply affected the surviving adventurers.

Shortly afterward, Orpheus joined the party and assisted in clearing the remaining dangers within the ruins.

The session permanently changed the tone of the campaign, shifting the story from simple adventure into something darker and far more dangerous.
