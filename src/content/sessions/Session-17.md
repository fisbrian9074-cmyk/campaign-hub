title: Shar's Gift
session_number: 17

summary: Orpheus loses his memories while the party becomes entangled with Triel's rebel factions.
---

After escaping the manor assault, the party retreated into the sewers beneath Triel.

There, Orpheus consumed the potion connected to Shar, unknowingly sacrificing his memories in the process.

The group later discovered the potion may originally have been intended to cure Eurydice.

Disoriented and vulnerable, Orpheus became increasingly susceptible to outside manipulation.

Meanwhile, the party encountered rebel groups operating beneath the city, including allies connected to Gwylyd, Brother Lewis, and the mysterious Green-Robed Elf.
