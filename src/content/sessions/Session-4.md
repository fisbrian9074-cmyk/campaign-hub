title: The Storm Beyond Greenest
session_number: 4
date: 2025-01-22T19:00:00.000-04:00
summary: A magical storm devastates the party during their travels near Greenest, leaving allies missing and new mysteries unfolding.
---

While traveling north from Greenest, the party became trapped within a violent magical storm unlike anything they had previously encountered.

When the storm finally passed, both Myla and Eluzara had vanished without explanation while the wizard Timmn suffered severe injuries.

The chaos surrounding the storm introduced the party to the drow paladin Inaphine, who joined the group shortly afterward.

The strange magical disturbances surrounding the event served as one of the earliest warnings that reality itself was becoming unstable.
