---
title: The Tavern Brawl
session_number: 1
date: 2026-05-06
summary: The party meets in the Rusty Dragon, but a disagreement over a spilled ale quickly turns into a full-scale brawl.
---

The adventure began not in a dungeon, but in the crowded common room of the Rusty Dragon. It was supposed to be a quiet evening.

However, when our Rogue attempted to pickpocket a visiting merchant, the ensuing scuffle knocked over the table of a rather large, rather angry Orc mercenary. Swords were drawn, tables were flipped, and the party was forced to work together to escape the city guard.
