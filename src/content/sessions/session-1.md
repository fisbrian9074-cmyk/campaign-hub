The campaign began with the party traveling by sea toward the mysterious Stormwreck Isle. Violent weather battered the vessel as strange magical disturbances surrounded the region.

Upon arriving at the island, the adventurers encountered the isolated cloister known as Dragon's Rest and met several of its inhabitants, including Runara, Tarak, and Varnoth.

The group quickly learned that Stormwreck Isle was far more dangerous than it first appeared. Rumors of undead sailors, strange magical events, and draconic activity spread throughout the island.

This session marked the true beginning of the party's shared journey.
