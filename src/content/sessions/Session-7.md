Following their victory in Berdusk, the party spent several months settling into their newfound status.

During this period, tensions across the realm worsened dramatically. Scornubel declared itself independent, trade routes became unstable, and Elturel attempted to maintain authority through increasingly aggressive military action.

Varrik eventually contacted the group again through Lord's Alliance channels, warning them that cult activity tied to Zariel continued spreading throughout the region.

The session reintroduced the wider political situation and prepared the party for the conflicts to come.
