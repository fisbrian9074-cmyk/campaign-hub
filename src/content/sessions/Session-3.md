---
title: The Fall of Sparkrender
session_number: 3
date: 2025-01-15T19:00:00.000-04:00
summary: The party confronts Sparkrender beneath Stormwreck Isle and earns the attention of infernal powers.
---

Deep beneath Stormwreck Isle, the party confronted Sparkrender and the dangerous forces destabilizing the island.

After a brutal battle, the group successfully defeated the dragon and prevented further destruction.

Their victory drew the attention of Vheamena, Kaithus' infernal patron from Avernus. She appeared before the party shortly after the battle, criticizing Kaithus for failing to reach his intended destination while simultaneously expressing admiration for the group's accomplishments.

Vheamena teleported the adventurers away from Stormwreck Isle and instructed them to travel north to meet a mysterious contact named Dak.

This event launched the party into the larger conspiracy surrounding the Broken Weave.
