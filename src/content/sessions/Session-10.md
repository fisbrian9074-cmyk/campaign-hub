---
title: The Roc
session_number: 10
date: 2025-08-22T19:00:00.000-04:00
summary: A monstrous roc attacks the party during their journey to Elturel, leading to the death of Gorruk Dragonbane Thorne.
---

While traveling near Fort Morninglord, the party was ambushed by a massive roc descending from the skies.

The creature smashed into the party's wagon, killing their horse and scattering the group.

During the battle, Gorruk was seized by the roc and carried high into the air. Even while trapped within the monster's grasp, he continued attacking until he finally delivered the killing blow.

The roc and Gorruk plummeted together from the sky.

Though the creature died, Gorruk did not survive the impact.

The surviving members of the party recovered the shattered remains of his greataxe and mourned the loss of one of their closest companions before continuing toward Elturel.
