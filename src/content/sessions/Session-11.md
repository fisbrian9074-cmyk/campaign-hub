title: The City Returned from Hell
session_number: 11
date: 2025-08-29T19:00:00.000-04:00
summary: The party arrives in Elturel and witnesses the scars left behind by the Descent.
---

After the death of Gorruk, the surviving adventurers continued their journey toward Elturel.

Along the road, they learned more about Fort Morninglord and the disappearances tied to the region before finally reaching the outskirts of the city.

Elturel revealed itself as a place permanently scarred by catastrophe. Massive infernal iron stakes surrounded the city while crumbling walls, broken towers, and refugee districts spread across the landscape.

The party learned details regarding the Descent — the month during which Elturel had vanished into the Nine Hells before mysteriously returning.

Escorted through the city gates under official summons, the adventurers prepared to stand before the Tribunal.
