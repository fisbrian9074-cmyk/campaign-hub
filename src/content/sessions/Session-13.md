The party spent time within the Tchazzum Estate following the Tribunal proceedings.

Apollo Tchazzum appeared surprisingly calm and welcoming toward Orpheus despite their strained history. The adventurers also met Lady Tabitha, Leto, and the estate's butler, Gilliam.

Elias confronted Apollo regarding suspicious financial records, hidden donations, and possible cult activity connected to the estate.

Although Apollo denied wrongdoing and attempted to explain past misunderstandings involving Orpheus and Eurydice, tensions within the group continued growing.

Later that night, Elias secretly recovered hidden documents from his old room while strange inconsistencies in the estate's behavior began drawing suspicion.
