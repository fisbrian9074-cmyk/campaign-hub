---

Following the death of General Strongsword, the party traveled back toward Elturel.

During the journey, Elias discovered that infernal iron caused his mysterious amulet to react violently, confirming similarities between the metal and the material fused into Corrinth's body.

Back at the Tchazzum Estate, the party secretly investigated Apollo's private office and uncovered extensive financial records showing sudden unexplained wealth, hidden accounts, and suspicious transfers spanning multiple cities.

Elias also recovered Apollo's personal journal, which described Father Henry performing a ritual that seemingly removed Apollo's guilt and emotional burdens regarding Eurydice, Orpheus, and his former wife.

Later, Apollo privately warned Orpheus about Shar's manipulative nature while admitting that parts of his own personality and memories no longer felt entirely natural.

The session ended with Orpheus effectively exiled from the estate while preparations for Leto's upcoming celebration continued.
