---
title: Summons to Elturel
session_number: 9
date: 2025-08-15T19:00:00.000-04:00
summary: The party receives an official summons from Elturel while darker warnings arrive from Varrik.
---

The party received two important letters.

The first, written partially in draconic, referenced "the name of the fallen" and hidden truths still waiting to be uncovered.

The second officially summoned the adventurers to Elturel, where the Tribunal wished to question them regarding the events in Berdusk and investigate rumors surrounding the group.

At the same time, Varrik warned the party that Zariel cultists had infiltrated multiple organizations throughout the region.

Realizing the growing scale of the conflict, the group departed for Elturel.
