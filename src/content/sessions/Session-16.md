title: The Black Iron Acolyte
session_number: 16
date: 2026-03-19T19:00:00.000-04:00
summary: The party discovers the horrifying truth hidden within Triel's corruption.
---

While investigating the source of Triel's growing instability, the party assaulted the manor connected to the city's leadership.

Inside, they encountered the terrifying Black Iron Acolyte — a figure of immense strength wreathed in infernal fire.

During the confrontation, the Acolyte removed her helmet and revealed herself to be Corrinth, Elias Viremont's wife.

Corrinth slaughtered the city's governoress before defeating the party with overwhelming force. Despite this, she spared their lives and abandoned them within the city square.

The revelation shattered Elias emotionally while simultaneously confirming that infernal forces tied to the Tchazzum conspiracy had transformed Corrinth into something unnatural.
