After arriving at Dragon's Rest, the party spent time learning about the island and its strange history.

The adventurers encountered kobolds, mysterious caves, and ancient draconic ruins while helping the cloister deal with growing dangers surrounding Stormwreck Isle.

The group slowly began bonding during these early adventures, forming the foundations of what would eventually become the Heroes of Berdusk.

At the same time, magical instability across the island became increasingly apparent.
