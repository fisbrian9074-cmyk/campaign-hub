Fort Morninglord is an old military stronghold located along the road to Elturel.

Roughly one hundred years ago, an entire detachment stationed at the fort mysteriously vanished without explanation. Since then, the location has remained sealed and heavily monitored.

During the party's journey toward Elturel, troops had recently reoccupied the surrounding encampments after being temporarily withdrawn to assist with regional conflicts.

The area surrounding Fort Morninglord became the site of Gorruk Dragonbane Thorne's final battle against a massive roc.
