The Haunted Ruins are a cursed ruin complex located near the village of Hornhollow.

For decades, the ruins spread madness, fear, and supernatural corruption across the surrounding countryside. Numerous adventuring groups and soldiers attempted to purge the location, including a company of Purple Dragon Knights from Cormyr.

Only one member of that expedition survived: Arthur, whose shattered mind left him wandering Hornhollow for nearly sixty years.

The party eventually explored the ruins, defeated the forces within, and lifted the curse haunting the area.

The ruins represented one of the party's earliest encounters with ancient supernatural corruption tied to the broader magical instability affecting the region.
