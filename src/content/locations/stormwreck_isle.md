---
name: Stormwreck Isle
type: Island
region: Trackless Sea
danger_level: High
---

Stormwreck Isle is a remote and storm-battered island located far from the mainland, known for its violent weather, ancient draconic ruins, and unstable magical energies.

The island served as the beginning of the party's journey, where they became entangled in conflicts involving dragons, kobolds, undead sailors, and ancient powers tied to Bahamut.

At the center of the island lies Dragon's Rest, a cloister overseen by the bronze dragon Runara. Though intended as a sanctuary for travelers and pilgrims, the island became increasingly unstable due to the influence of Sparkrender and the magical disturbances surrounding the region.

The party ultimately defeated Sparkrender upon Stormwreck Isle, an event that attracted the attention of the infernal patron Vheamena and set the adventurers upon the path toward the Broken Weave conspiracy.
