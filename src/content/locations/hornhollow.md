name: Hornhollow

region: Western Heartlands

---

Hornhollow is a small rural village haunted for decades by supernatural corruption originating from nearby ruins.

The settlement suffered from fear, disappearances, and strange behavior among its residents prior to the party's arrival.

The village barkeep had recently been murdered by Dak before the party entered town, though his death initially went unnoticed by most locals.

Hornhollow was also home to Arthur, a man believed to be insane who was later revealed to be a surviving Purple Dragon Knight trapped beneath the influence of the ruins' curse for nearly sixty years.

After the party successfully cleared the Haunted Ruins and ended the curse, the village slowly began recovering from generations of fear and isolation.
