Dragon's Rest is a peaceful cloister and sanctuary located upon Stormwreck Isle.

Overseen by the ancient bronze dragon Runara, the settlement serves as a refuge for travelers, pilgrims, and those seeking shelter from the dangers of the surrounding island.

The cloister contains a temple dedicated to Bahamut, modest living quarters, a trading post operated by Tarak, and several long-term residents including the retired mercenary Varnoth.

Despite its calm appearance, Dragon's Rest sits upon an island steeped in draconic conflict and magical instability. Strange events, shipwrecks, and violent storms frequently threaten the safety of those living there.

The location became the first true headquarters and safe haven for the party during the earliest days of their adventure.
