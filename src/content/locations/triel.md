Triel is a heavily contested city caught between military occupation, rebellion, refugee unrest, and cult infiltration.

Following Elturel's return from the Hells, Triel became overwhelmed with refugees and political instability. As tensions worsened, the city attempted to declare independence from Elturgard.

In response, General Strongsword led thousands of soldiers into the region under orders to restore order and eliminate suspected cult activity.

The city eventually descended into open conflict involving rebels, military forces, secret cultists, and covert agents tied to the Tchazzum conspiracy.

It was within Triel that Elias discovered Corrinth had become the terrifying Black Iron Acolyte, where Orpheus lost his memories through Shar's manipulation, and where the party ultimately assassinated General Strongsword.
