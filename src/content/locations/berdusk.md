Berdusk is a major city within Elturgard that became one of the earliest battlegrounds in the campaign's growing political and magical conflicts.

Under threat from cult activity and manipulation tied to the Order of the Lady of Iron, Berdusk descended into instability before the party intervened.

The adventurers ultimately defeated the city's ruling figure, Cierra Riv, an elven leader attempting to merge the Material Plane with the Feywild.

Following their victory, the party became celebrated as the Heroes of Berdusk and were granted land, titles, and property outside the city walls.

Months later, Berdusk remained politically tense as nearby powers struggled for control in the aftermath of Elturel's Descent.
