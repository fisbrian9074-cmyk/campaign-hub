Greenest is a small frontier settlement encountered by the party shortly after the events of Stormwreck Isle.

Though outwardly quiet, the town became the site of several mysterious events connected to magical instability and cult activity.

A violent magical storm struck the region during the party's travels near Greenest, resulting in the disappearance of Eluzara and Myla while leaving the wizard Timmn severely injured.

The settlement also served as one of the earliest locations where the party encountered Dak and forces connected to larger conspiracies spreading throughout Elturgard.
