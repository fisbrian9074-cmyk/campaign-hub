Elturel is the capital city of Elturgard and one of the most politically unstable locations in the region following the catastrophic event known as the Descent.

For one month, the city vanished into the Nine Hells before mysteriously returning to the Material Plane. Though physically restored, Elturel returned scarred, fractured, and deeply changed.

Massive infernal iron stakes surrounding the city serve as lingering reminders of the chains once used to drag Elturel into Avernus.

The city is now ruled by the Triad — leadership representing the gods Tyr, Torm, and Ilmater — who maintain order through military force, political investigations, and increasingly authoritarian measures.

Elturel is home to the Tchazzum Estate, numerous refugee districts, the Tribunal, and several hidden conspiracies tied to infernal corruption, Shar, and the Broken Weave.
