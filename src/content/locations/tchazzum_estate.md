The Tchazzum Estate is a lavish noble manor constructed within the recovering slums of Elturel following the city's return from the Hells.

Owned by Apollo Tchazzum and his family, the estate rapidly became one of the most suspicious and politically significant locations encountered by the party.

Behind its elegant exterior lies evidence of hidden financial manipulation, infernal correspondence, memory alteration, and secret dealings tied to the conspiracy surrounding the Broken Weave.

Elias Viremont formerly served as a legal assistant to the family before uncovering irregularities within Apollo's records and being dismissed.

The estate houses Apollo, Lady Tabitha, Leto, and the butler Gilliam, though multiple members of the party have noted strange behavior, missing memories, and unexplained magical phenomena occurring throughout the property.
